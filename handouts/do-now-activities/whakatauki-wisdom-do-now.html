Do Now: Whakataukī Wisdom

Unpacking the Wisdom of Māori Proverbs

What is a Whakataukī?

A whakataukī is a Māori proverb or saying. These short, clever statements contain generations of wisdom, offering guidance on how to live and insights into the Māori worldview. They are a powerful form of communication and a vital part of oral tradition.

Your Task: Interpret the Proverb

"Ehara taku toa i te toa takitahi, engari he toa takitini."

My strength is not as an individual, but as a collective.

Analysis Questions

1. In your own words, what does this whakataukī mean?

2. Describe a situation in your own life (at school, in sports, with friends, or at home) where this whakataukī would apply.

3. How does this proverb reflect a Te Ao Māori worldview? How might it be different from a more individualistic worldview?
