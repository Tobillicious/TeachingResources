Unit 7: Digital Technologies & AI Ethics

Navigating the Future of Artificial Intelligence

Unit Overview

This senior-level unit provides a critical and practical introduction to the world of Large Language Models (LLMs) and Artificial Intelligence. Students will not only learn how this technology works but will also grapple with the significant ethical questions it raises, including bias, security, and the vital concept of Māori Data Sovereignty.
