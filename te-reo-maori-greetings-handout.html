Basic Te Reo Māori Greetings

Everyday phrases for starting a conversation.

Greetings (Kupu Oha)

Kia ora

Pronounced: kee-ah oh-rah

Hello (A general, all-purpose greeting)

Mōrena

Pronounced: moh-reh-nah

Good morning

Kei te pēhea koe?

Pronounced: kay teh peh-heh-ah koh-eh

How are you?

Kei te pai

Pronounced: kay teh pie

I am good / Fine

Farewells (Kupu Poroporoaki)

Ka kite anō

Pronounced: kah kee-teh ah-noh

See you again

Haere rā

Pronounced: hah-eh-reh rah

Goodbye (said to someone leaving)

E noho rā

Pronounced: eh noh-hoh rah

Goodbye (said to someone staying)
