Lesson 3.1: How Does Local Government Work?

Year 8 Social Studies: How Systems Shape Our Lives

Lesson Activities

1. Introduction & Video (15 mins)

Introduce 'local government' (councils). Brainstorm what councils do (rubbish, parks, roads). Watch the 'What does your council do?' video.

2. Local Map Scavenger Hunt (20 mins)

Using a map of the local area, students work in pairs to find 3-5 examples of council-funded places or services (parks, libraries, pools, etc.).

3. "If I Were Mayor..." Poster (30 mins)

Students create a poster explaining one change they would make to improve their community for young people if they were mayor, including a drawing and a justification.

4. Gallery Walk (10 mins)

Students display their posters and walk around to see their classmates' ideas for improving the community.

Learning Goal

I can explain how local councils make decisions and who they affect.
