Lesson 2.1: What Kinds of Governments Exist?

Year 8 Social Studies: How Systems Shape Our Lives

Lesson Activities

1. Introduction (10 mins)

Introduce the concept of 'government' as the system that runs a country. Explain that there are many different types.

2. Station Rotation (30 mins)

In small groups, students rotate through stations, each with a card describing a type of government (Democracy, Monarchy, Dictatorship, etc.). At each station, they complete a PMI (Plus, Minus, Interesting) chart in their books.

3. Group Discussion & Vote (20 mins)

Groups discuss which system they would choose for a new country and why. Hold a class vote and create a bar chart of the results.

4. Exit Slip (15 mins)

Students answer in their books: "Which system of government do you think gives people the most rights? Explain why."

Learning Goal

I can describe different systems of government and how decisions are made.
