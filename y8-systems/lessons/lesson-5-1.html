Lesson 5.1: Plan Your Own System

Year 8 Social Studies: How Systems Shape Our Lives

Lesson Activities

1. Introduction to Summative Task (15 mins)

Introduce the final project: In groups, design a new, fair system to solve a problem in the school or community. Go over the assessment rubric.

2. Group Brainstorm and Planning (30 mins)

In groups, students choose a problem and use the 'Design a System' template to plan their system, considering its purpose, roles, rules, rights, and decision-making process.

3. Begin Creating Presentation (30 mins)

Groups begin creating a poster or digital slideshow to present their system to the class, explaining the problem and how their system solves it.

Learning Goal

I can design a fair system to solve a local problem and protect people’s rights.
