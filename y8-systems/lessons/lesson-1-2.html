Lesson 1.2: Rights and Responsibilities

Year 8 Social Studies: How Systems Shape Our Lives

Lesson Activities

1. Introduction & Video (15 mins)

Introduce the concepts of "Rights" (entitlements) and "Responsibilities" (duties). Watch the short video 'What Are Human Rights?' and discuss one right mentioned.

2. Gallery Walk (25 mins)

Post the 'Gallery Walk Statements' around the room. Students use sticky notes to 'Agree' or 'Disagree' with each statement, then discuss the different perspectives as a class.

3. T-Chart: Rights vs Responsibilities (20 mins)

Students create a T-chart in their books to list their rights and responsibilities at school. Challenge them to link a specific right to a corresponding responsibility.

4. Exit Slip (15 mins)

Students answer a final reflection question in their books: "Why is it important for rights and responsibilities to be balanced in a system?"

Learning Goal

I can explain what rights and responsibilities are and why they matter in a system.
