Lesson 2.2: Who Has Power?

Year 8 Social Studies: How Systems Shape Our Lives

Lesson Activities

1. Introduction & Video (10 mins)

Discuss what 'power' means in a system. Watch the short video 'Who Rules? Democracy vs Dictatorship' to compare two systems.

2. Roleplay Activity (35 mins)

Run a roleplay to decide on a class treat. First, as a 'Dictatorship' (teacher decides alone). Second, as a 'Monarchy' (teacher decides with a few advisors). Third, as a 'Democracy' (class votes).

3. Debrief and Discussion (20 mins)

In pairs and then as a class, students discuss which system felt the most and least fair, who had the power in each, and how it affected their rights.

4. Reflection Journal (10 mins)

Students write a reflection starting with: "I think the best system for making decisions is..." using examples from the roleplay.

Learning Goal

I can compare who has power in different systems and how that affects people’s rights.
