Lesson 4.2: Has the System Always Been Fair?

Year 8 Social Studies: How Systems Shape Our Lives

Lesson Activities

1. Jigsaw Reading (30 mins)

Students form 'expert' groups to read about one of three NZ protests (1975 Land March, 1981 Springbok Tour, Bastion Point). They then return to 'home' groups to teach their peers about their case study.

2. Creative Communication (25 mins)

In their home groups, students choose one of the protests and create either a protest poster or a comic strip that tells the story of the event.

3. Sharing and Reflection (20 mins)

Groups share their posters/comics. Students then write a reflection on one thing they learned about how people can challenge unfairness.

Learning Goal

I can explain how systems have changed and how people have challenged unfair ones.
