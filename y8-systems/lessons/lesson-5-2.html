Lesson 5.2: Present and Reflect

Year 8 Social Studies: How Systems Shape Our Lives

Lesson Activities

1. Group Presentations (40 mins)

Groups present their system designs to the class. The audience acts as a 'community panel', providing peer feedback using a "Two Stars and a Wish" model.

2. Peer Feedback Exchange (10 mins)

Groups read and discuss the feedback they received from their peers.

3. Final Self-Reflection (25 mins)

Students complete a final self-reflection in their workbooks, answering prompts about what they've learned, how their understanding has changed, and how they can participate in systems to make them fairer.

Learning Goal

I can explain how systems affect people and how we can take part in making them fairer.
