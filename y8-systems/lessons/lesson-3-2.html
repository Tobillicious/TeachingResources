Lesson 3.2: Who Gets a Say?

Year 8 Social Studies: How Systems Shape Our Lives

Lesson Activities

1. Shared Reading (25 mins)

Read "The Skate Park Campaign" case study as a class. Use a Think-Pair-Share strategy to identify the problem, the steps taken, and the outcome.

2. Mapping the System (20 mins)

Students draw a flowchart or mind map to show the steps the students in the story took to get their voices heard (e.g., identified problem -> gathered evidence -> contacted council -> presented case -> got result).

3. Plan a Class Campaign (20 mins)

As a class, choose a small school issue. In groups, students create a simple campaign plan: What is our goal? Who has the power to change it? What is our first step?

4. Exit Ticket (10 mins)

Students answer: "What is one way you can make your voice heard about an issue you care about?"

Learning Goal

I can explore how young people and communities make their voices heard.
