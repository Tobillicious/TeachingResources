Lesson 4.1: What is Te Tiriti o Waitangi?

Year 8 Social Studies: How Systems Shape Our Lives

Lesson Activities

1. Introduction & Video (15 mins)

Introduce Te Tiriti o Waitangi as the founding agreement of Aotearoa. Watch a short video about its history and purpose.

2. Sorting Activity (25 mins)

In groups, students sort cards with simplified Treaty articles, deciding whether each is a right/responsibility of the Crown (Kāwanatanga) or Māori (Rangatira). Discuss as a class.

3. Drawing 'Partnership' (20 mins)

Students draw a picture in their books that represents the concept of 'partnership', a key principle of the Treaty.

4. Reflection (15 mins)

Students answer in their books: "How is the Treaty of Waitangi a system of rights and responsibilities?"

Learning Goal

I can describe what the Treaty is and how it relates to rights and responsibilities in Aotearoa.
