NZC Level 5 - English & Social Sciences

Criteria Excellence Merit Achieved
Argument & Research Presents a sophisticated and insightful argument, supported by a range of well-integrated, credible evidence. Demonstrates a deep understanding of the issue's complexities. It masterfully employs a range of rhetorical devices (Ethos, Pathos, Logos) to create a powerful and memorable impact on the audience. Speech is well-structured and clearly delivered. Visual Text Visual text is highly impactful and original. Visual text is effective and well-designed. It uses visual techniques to convey a clear message that supports the speech. Visual text communicates a message that relates to the speech. It uses some basic visual techniques.

It expertly explains how and why specific persuasive techniques were chosen to affect the target audience. Rationale provides a clear explanation of the choices made in the campaign. It analyses how some persuasive techniques were used to affect the audience. Rationale describes the choices made in the campaign. It identifies some persuasive techniques used.
