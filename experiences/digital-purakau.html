🌟 Digital Pūrākau: Tāne and the Climate Challenge

Interactive Cultural Story - Kōrero Taiao

🔊 Karakia Tīmatanga: "Tēnā koutou katoa..."

Listen to the opening karakia before beginning this pūrākau

🌳 The Forest Guardian's Dilemma

English: In the time when the world was young, Tāne Mahuta, guardian of the forests, faced a great challenge. The balance of nature was shifting, and the earth was growing warmer. The trees whispered of strange weather patterns, and the birds spoke of disappearing habitats.

🔊 Te Reo Māori: "I nga rā o mua..."

Te Reo Māori: I ngā rā o mua, ka rongo a Tāne Mahuta i te tangi o ngā rākau. He huringa nui tēnei i te taiao. Ka kōrero ngā manu mō ō rātou kāinga e ngaro nei.

Traditional Wisdom: Tāne represents the intricate connection between all living things. In Māori cosmology, when one part of the natural world suffers, all parts feel the impact.

Today, a young rangatira named Aroha has received a vision from Tāne. She must choose how her iwi will respond to the changing climate. What path should she take?
