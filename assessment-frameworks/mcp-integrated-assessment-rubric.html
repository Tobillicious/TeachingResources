ü§ù Collaborative Excellence Framework

Cross-Curricular Integration

  • Excellence: Seamlessly integrates 4+ learning areas with sophisticated connections
  • Merit: Clearly integrates 3 learning areas with good connections
  • Achieved: Integrates 2 learning areas with basic connections
  • Not Yet: Single learning area focus with no integration

Community Impact

  • Excellence: Demonstrates clear positive community impact with authentic engagement
  • Merit: Shows potential community impact with some engagement
  • Achieved: Considers community impact with limited engagement
  • Not Yet: No consideration of community impact
